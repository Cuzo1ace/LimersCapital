# Limer's Capital — 3-Minute Pitch Video Script

> **Target**: Colosseum Frontier Hackathon judges + investors
> **Duration**: 3:00 (aim for 2:50 to leave breathing room)
> **Tone**: Confident, clear, Caribbean pride, data-backed
> **Speaker**: Founder (on camera or voiceover with screen recording)

---

## [0:00 – 0:20] HOOK — The Problem

**[Visual: Map of the Caribbean, highlighting T&T, Jamaica, Barbados]**

"700 million people live in the Caribbean and Latin America — but less than 4% have ever touched a crypto wallet. Not because they don't want to. Because nothing was built for them.

The exchanges speak English. The education assumes Wall Street. The on-ramps don't accept TTD or JMD. And the result? An entire region locked out of the fastest-growing asset class in history."

---

## [0:20 – 0:50] THE SOLUTION

**[Visual: Limer's Capital dashboard loading — dark theme, Caribbean colors]**

"Limer's Capital is the Caribbean's first DeFi education and trading platform on Solana.

We don't just give you a chart and say 'good luck.' We teach you — from blockchain basics to advanced LP strategies — then let you practice with zero-risk paper trading before you touch a single dollar.

Think Duolingo meets Robinhood, built on the fastest chain in crypto."

---

## [0:50 – 1:25] HOW IT WORKS

**[Visual: Quick screen recording of the Learn → Trade → Earn flow]**

"Here's how it works:

**Learn** — 8 structured modules, 40 lessons, 8 quizzes. From 'What is a blockchain?' to 'How to optimize LP positions on Meteora.' Every quiz you pass unlocks new features and earns you XP.

**Trade** — Paper trade 14 Solana tokens and 30 Caribbean stocks from the Trinidad & Tobago Stock Exchange. Practice limit orders, stop losses, even perpetual futures — all with simulated funds. When you're ready, execute real on-chain swaps through Jupiter.

**Earn** — Every action earns Limer Points. Complete lessons, execute trades, maintain streaks, refer friends. Points convert to $LIMER token allocation at launch. We have 25 achievement badges, a 10-tier ranking system, and live trading competitions."

---

## [1:25 – 1:55] MARKET OPPORTUNITY

**[Visual: TAM/SAM/SOM slide from pitch deck]**

"Caribbean fintech is a 1.2 billion dollar market growing at 18.5% annually. There are zero — zero — blockchain consumer products targeting the region today.

Trinidad and Tobago just passed the Virtual Asset Service Provider Act in 2025, creating the first regulated crypto framework in the English-speaking Caribbean. Our fiat on-ramp partner, Wam, lets users convert TTD to crypto locally.

We're not competing with Binance or Coinbase. We're serving a market they've completely ignored."

---

## [1:55 – 2:25] TRACTION & TECH

**[Visual: Architecture diagram + Solana integration]**

"We're live on Solana devnet and mainnet. Wallet integration with Solflare and Phantom. Real Jupiter V6 swaps. Meteora pool data powering our AI LP analyzer — Agent Squeeze — which scores liquidity pools and detects portfolio risk.

The platform has 17 pages, 189 passing tests, i18n in three languages, and a scoring engine for trading competitions. We built a perpetuals simulator with proper liquidation math, funding rates, and P&L tracking.

Our on-chain program stores user profiles, badges, and trade history on Solana — creating a verifiable, portable credential for financial literacy."

Phase 1 is live — the learning and trading platform. Phase 2 launches the $LIMER token with staking tiers and revenue sharing. Phase 3 opens institutional API access and cross-chain expansion.

We're a team that understands this market because we come from this market. Limer's Capital isn't just a product — it's an on-ramp for an entire region into the future of finance.

Thank you."
