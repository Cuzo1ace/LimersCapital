### THE OPPORTUNITY

The TTSE lists ~US$21B in market capitalization of fundamentally sound Caribbean companies — and trades them at a **9.9x PE, a 42% discount to the emerging-market average**. This is not a quality problem; it is a plumbing problem. Illiquidity, fragmentation, information asymmetry, and access barriers suppress valuations across the entire Caribbean capital markets ecosystem.

Meanwhile, the world's largest capital markets are modernizing onto programmable settlement rails. Securitize, Ondo Finance, WisdomTree, and the xStocks consortium are already issuing tokenized US and European equities at institutional scale, with full SEC / FINMA / BaFin oversight. **Trinidad & Tobago can lead CARICOM into this transition — or be left behind by it.**

### OUR PROPOSAL

Limer's Capital proposes a **strategic technology partnership** in which we build, secure, and operate a regulator-sanctioned tokenization layer for TTSE-listed equities. Critically:

- **TTSE remains the registry of record.** Every tokenized share is backed 1:1 by an underlying share locked in TTSE's designated depository and reconciled daily.
- **TTSEC retains full regulatory authority.** All issuances occur under existing Securities Act frameworks with no new regulatory regime required.
- **Limer's Capital is the technology provider, not a competing exchange.** We operate the rails; the exchange operates the market.

This is not a new venue. It is a modernization layer that sits beneath the existing market and makes it globally accessible.

### HOW IT WORKS — REGULATOR'S VIEW

1. **Shares stay at TTSE.** For every tokenized share issued on-chain, an equivalent underlying share is immobilized in regulated custody. Daily 1:1 reconciliation reports are delivered automatically to TTSE and TTSEC.
2. **KYC is a prerequisite to holding.** No wallet can receive a tokenized TTSE share without first passing identity verification through a TTSEC-approved provider. New accounts are **frozen by default** until cleared — matching the enhanced-due-diligence model used by Securitize.
3. **Every transfer is compliance-checked at the protocol level.** Jurisdictional rules, sanctions screening, and suitability checks execute automatically on every single trade. Non-compliant transfers cannot settle.
4. **Clawback and freeze are preserved.** TTSE and TTSEC retain unilateral ability to freeze, reverse, or recover tokens via multi-signature controls for court orders, market integrity actions, or fraud response.
5. **Complete audit trail.** Every transaction is permanently recorded and exported daily in a registry-compatible format. Beneficial ownership is traceable in real time, exceeding CARICOM AML requirements.

### SAFEGUARDS & CYBERSECURITY COMMITMENTS

Limer's Capital will build and operate this infrastructure under institutional-grade security standards:

- **Two independent smart-contract audits** by Tier-1 Solana security firms prior to any mainnet deployment, and annually thereafter
- **Permanent bug bounty program** scaled to total value at risk
- **HSM-backed multi-signature key management** for all mint, freeze, and clawback authorities — with a mandatory TTSE signer on every privileged action
- **Real-time on-chain surveillance** integrated with AML/KYT providers (Chainalysis or TRM Labs) for sanctions and illicit-finance screening
- **Global pause switch** operable unilaterally by TTSE for market integrity interventions
- **Incident response aligned to TTSEC's breach-notification timelines** (24/72 hour) with pre-approved communication templates
- **Penetration testing** of the web and API layers quarterly by an independent firm
- **Open-source foundation** built on Solana Token-2022 extensions, Solana Attestation Service, and the Upside RWA program — the same open stack used by Securitize, Ondo, and the xStocks consortium, not proprietary code
