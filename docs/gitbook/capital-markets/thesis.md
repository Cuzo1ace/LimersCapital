Limer's Capital positions itself as the on-chain gateway connecting three layers of capital markets through a single Solana wallet.

**Status**: Live (paper trading)

What's available today:
- 30+ ordinary shares and preference shares
- 5 market indices (Composite, All T&T, Cross-Listed, SME, USD)

- TTSEC regulatory compliance mapping

**Status**: Expanding (12-18 months)

The Caribbean has 5+ separate stock exchanges operating under different regulatory frameworks, currencies, settlement systems, and trading hours. Cross-listed stocks show 5% price differentials between exchanges. CARICOM has pursued integration for 20+ years with limited success.

What we're building:
- Jamaica Stock Exchange (JSE) integration — 45+ companies, US$12.2B market cap
- Barbados Stock Exchange (BSE) — 20 companies, US$2.75B market cap
- Eastern Caribbean Securities Exchange (ECSE) — 8 ECCU nations
- Cross-border settlement via Solana eliminating price differentials
- Caribbean Bond Market access (government + corporate)
- CBDC interoperability layer (Sand Dollar, JAM-DEX, DCash)

Combined Caribbean equities: **~US$37B** currently fragmented across 5 exchanges.
