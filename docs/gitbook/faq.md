# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Limer's Capital?

Limer's Capital is the Caribbean's first DeFi education-and-trading platform on Solana. It connects local Caribbean capital markets to regional and global markets through a single wallet -- learn, trade, earn, and grow all in one place. [Read more](platform/overview.md).

## Is it free?

Yes. Education is always free -- this is enshrined in the irrevocable [Protocol Charter](tokenomics/ubo.md). Paper trading is free. Real swaps through Jupiter incur standard network fees. Premium features (advanced analytics, API access) will be available via [staking tiers](tokenomics/staking-tiers.md).

## What is $LIMER?

$LIMER is the native utility and governance token of Limer's Capital. Total supply is 1 billion tokens with a 50/50 community/protocol split. It is used for governance voting, staking for revenue share, and unlocking premium features. [Full details](tokenomics/limer-token.md).

## When does the token launch?

The $LIMER token generation event (TGE) date has not been announced. Limer Points (LP) earned on the platform will convert to $LIMER tokens at launch. Follow [@limerscapital](https://x.com/limerscapital) for updates.

## Is my money safe?

Limer's Capital never custodies your funds. All assets remain in your self-custodial wallet. Paper trading uses simulated funds with zero real risk. Real swaps are executed on-chain through Jupiter -- you control your keys at all times.

## What wallets are supported?

Any Solana wallet that supports the wallet-standard, including Solflare (primary partner), Phantom, Backpack, and others. Connect your wallet to access real swaps and on-chain features.

## What is UBO?

Universal Basic Ownership is our founding economic model: 50% of tokens go to the community, 50% of revenue goes to stakers (paid in USDC and SOL), and education stays free forever. [Full UBO details](tokenomics/ubo.md).

## How do I earn LP?

Complete lessons, pass quizzes, execute trades, maintain daily streaks, refer friends, and participate in trading competitions. Every action on the platform earns Limer Points. [See the Earn system](platform/earn.md).

## What is the TTSE?

The Trinidad & Tobago Stock Exchange -- the primary equity market for Trinidad & Tobago. It trades at a 9.9x PE ratio, significantly undervalued compared to regional and global peers. Limer's Capital offers paper trading of ~30 TTSE-listed stocks. [Read the analysis](capital-markets/ttse-undervaluation.md).

## How do I get started?

Visit [limerscapital.com](https://limerscapital.com), connect a Solana wallet, and start with the first education module. No signup, no KYC, no fees -- just connect and learn.
