# Partnerships

Limer's Capital partners with established infrastructure providers to deliver a seamless Caribbean DeFi experience.

## Active Partners

### Wam -- TTD On-Ramp

Wam is a VASP-licensed fiat on-ramp approved by the Central Bank of Trinidad & Tobago. Wam handles KYC/AML compliance and enables direct TTD-to-crypto conversions, solving the critical last-mile problem for Caribbean users entering DeFi.

### Solflare -- Primary Wallet Partner

Solflare is the primary wallet integration for Limer's Capital. A 2% token allocation is reserved for the Wallet Boost program -- rewarding users who connect and transact through Solflare.

### Jupiter -- Swap Aggregation

Jupiter V6 powers all real on-chain swaps on the platform. Users get optimal routing across Solana DEXs with the same interface they practiced on in paper trading mode.

### Meteora -- LP Pool Data

Meteora provides liquidity pool data used by the Agent Squeeze analytics engine, giving users visibility into LP opportunities across the Solana ecosystem.
