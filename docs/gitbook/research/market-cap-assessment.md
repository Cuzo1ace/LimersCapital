# Market Cap Assessment

This page summarizes the Limer's Capital Market Cap Assessment. For the complete 12-page analyst report, see the PDF version in our GitHub repository at `docs/Limers-Capital-Market-Cap-Assessment.pdf`.

## Executive Summary

Limer's Capital occupies a unique position as the only DeFi education-and-trading platform targeting the Caribbean region on Solana. With zero direct competitors, a live production platform (19 pages, 361 tests), and a structurally undervalued target market (TTSE at 9.9x PE), the project presents a differentiated value proposition in the Solana ecosystem.

## Valuation Methodology

The assessment uses a probability-weighted scenario analysis across three cases: bear ($25M FDV), base ($50M FDV), and bull ($100M FDV), weighted by execution probability and market conditions. The ~$42M probability-weighted estimate reflects the early stage and solo-founder risk balanced against zero competition and structural market opportunity.
