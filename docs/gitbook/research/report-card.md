# Colosseum Report Card

Limer's Capital was benchmarked against Colosseum hackathon judging criteria in March 2026, scoring 8.2/10 overall.

## Category Breakdown

| Category | Grade | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **Gamified Education** | B+ | 37 lessons and 8 quizzes; room for adaptive learning and video content |
| **Stock Tokenization** | A | TTSE paper trading live; RWA tokenization roadmap well-defined |
| **LP/DeFi Tools** | A- | Jupiter integration, Meteora data; staking and LP farming pending |
| **Gamified Trading** | A | 4 trading modes, competitions, 5-metric scoring engine |
| **Regional Focus** | A+ | Only platform targeting 22 Caribbean jurisdictions |
| **Regulatory Mapping** | A+ | Comprehensive coverage across all regulatory statuses |
| **Security** | B+ | API keys on Workers, CSP headers, wallet-standard; multi-sig pending |

## Head-to-Head Summaries

**vs Rekt**: Rekt offers news and post-mortems but no trading or education tools. Limer's Capital provides a complete learn-to-trade pipeline.

**vs The Arena**: The Arena offers gamified trading competitions but lacks education, Caribbean focus, and RWA tokenization.

**vs Trenchers**: Trenchers targets memecoin trading with leaderboards but has no education system, no regional market coverage, and no regulatory mapping.

**vs LEarn**: LEarn provides basic crypto education with XP rewards but lacks trading features, Caribbean market data, and the depth of Limer's 8-module curriculum.
