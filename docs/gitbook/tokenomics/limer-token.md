# $LIMER Token

$LIMER is the native utility and governance token of Limer's Capital. Total supply is fixed at 1 billion tokens with a 50/50 split between community and protocol.

## Token Distribution

### Community (50% -- 500M tokens)

| Allocation | % of Supply | Tokens | Purpose |
|-----------|-------------|--------|---------|
| Airdrops | 18% | 180M | Reward early platform users and LP earners |
| LP Mining | 12% | 120M | Ongoing rewards for platform engagement |
| Referrals | 10% | 100M | User-to-user growth incentives |
| Education | 10% | 100M | Earned through lesson and quiz completion |

### Protocol (50% -- 500M tokens)

| Allocation | % of Supply | Tokens | Purpose |
|-----------|-------------|--------|---------|
| Treasury | 15% | 150M | Protocol reserves and operational runway |
| Team | 15% | 150M | Founder and future team compensation |
| Development | 10% | 100M | Engineering, audits, infrastructure |
| Partnerships | 10% | 100M | Exchange listings, integrations, ecosystem growth |

## Vesting Schedules

| Allocation | Cliff | Vesting Period | Schedule |
|-----------|-------|----------------|----------|
| Team | 12 months | 36 months | Linear monthly unlock |
| Treasury | None | 48 months | Quarterly unlock, governance-approved |
| Partnerships | 6 months | 24 months | Linear monthly unlock |
| Community (airdrops) | None | Immediate at TGE | One-time distribution |
| LP Mining | None | 48 months | Emission curve (front-loaded) |

## Utility

$LIMER serves three functions: governance voting on protocol decisions, staking for revenue share and fee discounts, and access to premium platform features.
