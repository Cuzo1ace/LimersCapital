# Revenue Streams

Limer's Capital generates revenue through 7 diversified streams, with 50% of all revenue flowing to $LIMER stakers via [Universal Basic Ownership](ubo.md).

## Revenue Breakdown

| Stream | Share | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Spot Trading Fees** | 30% | Fees on Jupiter-routed real swaps executed through the platform |
| **Perpetuals Fees** | 25% | Trading fees on the perpetuals engine (post-launch) |
| **TTSE Listing Fees** | 15% | Fees for tokenized Caribbean equity listings and secondary trading |
| **Premium Subscriptions** | 10% | Advanced analytics, portfolio tools, institutional-grade data |
| **Institutional API** | 10% | Programmatic access to Caribbean market data and execution |
| **Referrals** | 5% | Revenue share from partner integrations and user referrals |
| **Bridge Conversions** | 5% | Fees on fiat-to-crypto conversions via Wam TTD on-ramp |

## Revenue Flow

```
User Activity

    v
Platform Revenue (7 streams)

    |

    |
    +--> 20% to Growth (partnerships, marketing)
```
