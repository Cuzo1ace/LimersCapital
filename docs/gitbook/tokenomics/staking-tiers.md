# Staking Tiers

Staking $LIMER tokens unlocks progressive benefits across four tiers. All stakers receive revenue share through [Universal Basic Ownership](ubo.md); higher tiers add fee discounts, governance weight, and premium features.

## Tier Overview

| Tier | $LIMER Required | Fee Discount | LP Multiplier | Governance Weight |
|------|----------------|--------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Bronze** | 1,000 | 10% | 1.1x | 1x |
| **Silver** | 10,000 | 20% | 1.25x | 2x |
| **Gold** | 100,000 | 35% | 1.5x | 4x |
| **Platinum** | 1,000,000 | 50% | 2.0x | 8x |

## Tier Benefits

### Bronze (1,000 $LIMER)
- 10% trading fee discount
- 1.1x LP earning multiplier
- Access to basic analytics dashboard
- Revenue share participation

### Silver (10,000 $LIMER)
- 20% trading fee discount
- 1.25x LP earning multiplier
- Advanced charting and portfolio tools
- Priority customer support

### Gold (100,000 $LIMER)
- 35% trading fee discount
- 1.5x LP earning multiplier
- Institutional-grade market data
- Early access to new features
- 4x governance voting weight

### Platinum (1,000,000 $LIMER)
- 50% trading fee discount
- 2.0x LP earning multiplier
- API access for programmatic trading
- Direct team communication channel
- 8x governance voting weight

## Staking Mechanics

Staked tokens are locked for a minimum of 7 days. Unstaking initiates a 7-day cooldown period. Revenue share is distributed weekly in USDC and SOL.
