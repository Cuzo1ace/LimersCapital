Limer's Capital roadmap is organized in four horizons, moving from a live platform to full Caribbean capital market integration.

## NOW -- Live Platform

- 19 functional pages, 361 passing tests across 14 test files
- 8 education modules, 37 lessons, 8 server-validated quizzes
- Paper trading: 14 Solana tokens + ~30 TTSE stocks
- Perpetuals simulator with 20x leverage
- Real Jupiter V6 swaps
- 22 Caribbean jurisdictions mapped

## 24 MONTHS

- **Remittance corridor** -- diaspora-to-Caribbean crypto rails with sub-1% fees
- **CBDC integration** -- JAM-DEX and DCash interoperability

- **1.8M MAU target** -- scaling through regional expansion and diaspora network effects
