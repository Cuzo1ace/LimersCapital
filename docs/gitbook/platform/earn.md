# Earn -- $LIMER Points & Gamification

Every action on Limer's Capital earns Limer Points (LP) and XP. Points convert to $LIMER tokens at launch, rewarding early adopters who learn, trade, and engage.

## 25 Achievement Badges

Badges unlock across four categories: Education (8), Trading (8), Community (5), and Streaks (4). Each badge awards a one-time LP bonus and appears on the user's public profile.

## Earning Actions

- Complete lessons and quizzes
- Execute paper trades and real swaps
- Maintain daily login streaks
- Refer new users
- Participate in trading competitions

## Multipliers

- **Daily streak multiplier**: 1.1x at 7 days, 1.25x at 30 days, 1.5x at 90 days
- **Tier multiplier**: higher ranks earn LP faster
- **Competition multiplier**: active competitions boost all LP earned by 2x

## Trading Competitions

Competitions use a 5-metric scoring engine: total PnL, win rate, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, and trade count. Leaderboards update in real time with prizes distributed in LP.
