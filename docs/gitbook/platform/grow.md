# Grow -- RWA Tokenization & Universal Basic Ownership

Grow is the long vision for Limer's Capital -- tokenizing Caribbean real-world assets and pioneering Universal Basic Ownership (UBO) for the region.

## The RWA Tokenization Roadmap

Asset tokenization follows a phased approach, starting with the most liquid and regulated asset classes:

| Phase | Asset Class | Example | Timeline |
|-------|------------|---------|----------|
| 1 | Equities | TTSE-listed stocks (ANSA McAL, Republic Bank) | 12-24 months |
| 2 | Real Estate | Caribbean commercial and residential property | 24-36 months |
| 3 | Fixed Income | Government and corporate bonds | 36-48 months |
| 4 | Insurance | Climate and catastrophe insurance products | 48+ months |

## Universal Basic Ownership

UBO is a new ownership model designed for the Caribbean:

- **50% of $LIMER tokens** go to the community -- not VCs, not insiders
- **50% of protocol revenue** flows to stakers in USDC and SOL (never in $LIMER to avoid circular dilution)
- **Education is always free** -- the Protocol Charter makes this irrevocable
- **No entity may hold more than 25% voting power** -- preventing capture

## The Diaspora as Shareholders

6 million Caribbean-born people live abroad. UBO transforms diaspora remittances from a cost center (7% average fees) into an investment channel. Instead of sending money home, diaspora members become shareholders in their region's financial infrastructure.
