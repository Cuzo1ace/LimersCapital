# What is Limer's Capital?

Limer's Capital is the Caribbean's first DeFi education-and-trading platform on Solana. We're building the on-chain gateway that connects local Caribbean capital markets to regional exchanges and global markets — all through a single wallet.

## The Problem We Solve

700M+ Caribbean and Latin American people are locked out of the global crypto economy. Fewer than 4% have ever touched a wallet — not for lack of interest, but because:

- The exchanges speak Wall Street English
- On-ramps don't accept TTD, JMD, or XCD
- Zero native DeFi platforms target the 22 Caribbean nations
- $20B in annual remittances bleeds 7% in fees

Meanwhile, Caribbean equities on the TTSE trade at a **9.9x PE ratio** — 42% below emerging market averages — due to structural problems (illiquidity, fragmentation, no institutional access) that blockchain technology can solve.

## Our Approach: The Four-Pillar Pipeline

### 1. Learn
We meet users where they are. Absolute beginners start with "What is a blockchain?" and progress through 8 structured modules, 37 lessons, and 8 server-validated quizzes covering blockchain fundamentals, Caribbean markets, Solana DeFi, LP strategies, and Universal Basic Ownership.

### 2. Trade
Users practice with zero risk before committing real capital. Paper trade 14 Solana tokens and 30 TTSE Caribbean stocks with $100K virtual USD and TT$679K virtual TTD. The perpetuals simulator offers up to 20x leverage with competition-grade liquidation math. When ready, execute real on-chain swaps via Jupiter V6.

### 3. Earn
Every action earns Limer Points (LP) and XP. Complete lessons, execute trades, maintain streaks, refer friends. The platform has 25 achievement badges, a 10-tier ranking system (Sand Walker → Leviathan), LP multipliers, and live trading competitions. Points convert to $LIMER tokens at launch.

### 4. Grow
The long vision: tokenize Caribbean real-world assets — TTSE stocks, real estate, bonds, climate insurance — and pioneer Universal Basic Ownership (UBO) for the region. 50% of $LIMER tokens go to the community. 50% of protocol revenue goes to stakers. The Caribbean diaspora as shareholders in their region's financial future.
